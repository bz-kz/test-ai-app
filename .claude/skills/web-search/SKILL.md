Returns a summary of search results for the specified query. Provide a clear explanation of why the search is needed and how its results will be used to inform agent actions. You can use this skill to gather information about a topic, search for resources, or research specific questions that arise during a task. When you use this skill, you specify the search query and why you want web search results. Always ensure that searches are task-relevant and that the results are used to enhance agent performance and decision-making.
